Overview

ResQ overarching goal is to redefine pedestrian safety through innovative and multifaceted platforms.

ResQ is a paradigm shift in pedestrian safety by converging state of the art UAV with web and mobile applications by utilizing AGI along with Python, Flask, JavaScript, PostgreSQL and Pydroid to ensure astute and proactive accident response and prevention.

Approach

The development approach centers on crafting an intuitive Pydroid app for incident reporting empowering individuals and a web application equipped with advanced tools for rescue teams.A shared backend ensures synchronized data promoting a cohesive response to pedestrian safety incidents.

ResQ can elevate the entire accident scenario predictions by facilitating efficient on-site decision-making. It estimates patient survival time, assesses intensity by suggesting immediate first aid and generates detailed reports thereby enhancing decision-making in emergency situations.
